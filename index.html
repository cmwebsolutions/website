FAQ

Clear answers before you commit.

If you are unsure whether the subscription model is right for your business, book a free consultation and we’ll talk through it.

Is VAT added to the £149/month?

No. We are not currently VAT registered, so VAT is not added to your monthly fee.

Is there a minimum term?

Yes. The website subscription has a 12-month minimum term. This allows the design, build, launch and support to be spread across the subscription.

Who owns the website?

Ownership terms are confirmed in your agreement. A common approach is that the website transfers after the minimum term is complete and payments are up to date.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. If you need extra pages, ecommerce, booking systems, landing pages or ongoing marketing support, we can move you onto a more suitable plan.

What counts as support?

Support usually includes hosting-related help, basic maintenance, updates and reasonable minor changes. Larger new features or new pages may be quoted separately.

Is ongoing SEO included?

Basic SEO setup is included. Ongoing SEO campaigns, content creation, technical SEO and link building are separate services.
