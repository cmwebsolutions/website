## Your website is your digital shopfront

Think about the last time you searched for a local service. You probably opened Google, clicked the first result, and decided within seconds whether that business was worth your time. That's the power of a website — and why getting it right matters.

### What makes a good website?

A good website isn't just about looking pretty. It needs to:

- **Load fast** — visitors expect a site to load in under 3 seconds
- **Be easy to navigate** — if they can't find what they need, they'll leave
- **Work on every device** — over 60% of web traffic comes from mobile
- **Communicate clearly** — visitors should know what you offer within seconds

### The cost of a bad website

A poorly designed website costs you more than just the price tag. It costs you:

- Lost leads from slow load times
- Missed sales from confusing navigation
- Damaged credibility from an outdated design

Investing in a proper website isn't an expense — it's one of the best marketing investments you can make.
