## Your website is your digital shopfront

Think about the last time you searched for a local service. You opened Google, clicked the first result, and decided within seconds whether that business was worth your time. That's what happens every single day with your own website.

A professionally designed site does more than look good. It builds trust, guides visitors where you want them to go, and works quietly in the background to bring in leads - even while you're asleep.

### What makes a good website?

There's no magic formula, but every effective website we've built shares a few things:

- **It loads fast** - under 3 seconds or people leave. Simple as that.
- **It's easy to navigate** - if someone can't find your services page in two clicks, you're losing business
- **It works on every device** - more than 60% of traffic comes from mobile, and that number keeps growing
- **It's clear** - a visitor should know what you do within seconds of landing

### What a bad website costs you

A poor website doesn't just look bad - it actively costs you money. Slow load times cost you leads. Confusing navigation costs you sales. An outdated design makes people question whether you're still in business.

We've had clients come to us after their old site was doing more harm than good. One was losing quote requests because their contact form simply didn't work on mobile. Simple fix, massive difference.

A proper website isn't an expense - it's one of the best marketing investments you can make.
